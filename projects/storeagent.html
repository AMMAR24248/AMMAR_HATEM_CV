Featured Project

StoreAgent SaaS

AI-Powered Commerce Assistant

StoreAgent is a software platform designed to help online store owners manage customer messages, extract orders automatically, and organize business operations through a smart backend system.

Project Overview

The system receives customer messages from social media platforms, analyzes the message content, extracts order details such as product, quantity, phone number, and address, then stores the order inside a PostgreSQL database.

Problem

Many small online stores receive a large number of customer messages manually. This causes delays, missed orders, repeated questions, and difficulty tracking customer requests.

Solution

StoreAgent automates the first stage of customer communication by reading messages, detecting order intent, generating replies, and saving complete order information for the store owner.
